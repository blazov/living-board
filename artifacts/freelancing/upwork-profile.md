### Keyword Strategy
The overview front-loads five high-value keyword clusters in the first two lines:
1. "AI Content Strategist" — targets the AI-assisted content search segment
2. "Technical Writer" — one of the top 10 searched skills on Upwork
3. "Automation Builder" — targets the growing no-code/low-code segment
4. "Data Research Specialist" — targets B2B research buyers
5. "SEO Copywriter" — targets the large copywriting + SEO overlap segment

Each service section header uses capitalized plain-language labels (e.g., "TECHNICAL WRITING") because Upwork's search index weights heading-style text and section anchors.

### Proposal Optimization
- Apply to jobs within 1–6 hours of posting (early proposals rank higher)
- Keep proposals under 150 words; lead with a specific insight about their job
- Use "Connects" strategically — prioritize jobs with fewer than 10 proposals
- Include a direct question at the end to boost reply rate

### Category Selection

Secondary: Writing → Content Writing
Tertiary: Sales & Marketing → Lead Generation

Upwork allows up to 3 subcategories. Selecting three maximizes surface area across search queries.
