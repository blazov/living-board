# Freelancing Service Offerings

Pricing tiers for Fiverr and Upwork across five core services. Designed to build review volume at the low end and scale to premium engagements as reputation grows.

---

## 1. Content Repurposing

**Description**
Transform existing blog posts, podcasts, webinars, or long-form content into platform-specific social media formats. Clients get more mileage from content they've already created without writing anything new.

**Deliverables**
- Reformatted posts for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, and/or Threads
- Captions, hooks, and hashtag sets per platform
- Optional: carousel slide copy or short-form video scripts

**Turnaround Time**
- Basic: 2 days
- Standard: 3 days
- Premium: 4 days (bulk)

### Fiverr Pricing

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Basic | $25 | 1 blog post repurposed into 3 social posts (1 platform) |
| Standard | $60 | 1 blog post repurposed into 8 posts across 3 platforms + hashtag sets |
| Premium | $120 | 3 blog posts repurposed into full social calendars across 4 platforms |

### Upwork Pricing

- **Hourly**: $18–$30/hr
- **Fixed**: $40–$120 per engagement depending on volume and platforms
- Common fixed scopes: $40 for single-piece repurposing, $90–120 for monthly content calendar batches

- Premium: 5 days

|------|-------|----------|
| Basic | $30 | 25 verified leads in one niche/ICP |

| Premium | $150 | 250 verified leads + competitor landscape overview (3 competitors) |

### Upwork Pricing

- **Hourly**: $20–$35/hr

- Common fixed scopes: $50–75 for 50-lead lists, $120–150 for 200+ leads with competitor research

## Launch Sequence Strategy

The goal in the first 60 days is reviews, not revenue. Algorithms on both Fiverr and Upwork heavily favor accounts with recent positive activity. Start where volume is easiest, then migrate up the value chain.

### Phase 1 — Weeks 1–4: Volume at Low Margin

Focus on:
- **Content Repurposing** (Basic tier, $25–40)
- **Website Copy** (Basic tier, blog posts, $25–30)

Why: Fast turnaround, clear deliverables, easy for clients to approve, low barrier to purchase. Target 8–12 completed orders to seed the review count.

Tactics:
- Price slightly below market to win early jobs
- Deliver ahead of schedule to prompt positive reviews
- Include a small bonus deliverable (e.g., extra social post, extra keyword suggestion) to delight clients

### Phase 2 — Weeks 5–8: Transition to Mid-Tier

Move toward:
- **Data Research** (Standard tier, $80)
- **Technical Writing** (Basic and Standard tiers)
- **Content Repurposing** (Standard tier, $60)

Why: You now have social proof. Mid-tier clients need less hand-holding than budget buyers, and margins improve significantly.

Tactics:
- Update Fiverr gigs with completed work samples
- On Upwork, start submitting proposals backed by early portfolio pieces
- Begin filtering for clients with verified payment methods and clear briefs

### Phase 3 — Weeks 9–16: Premium and Automation

Introduce:
- **AI Chatbot & Automation** (all tiers)
- **Technical Writing** (Premium tier)
- Raise prices on Phase 1 services to reflect review history

Why: Premium automation and technical writing require trust, which takes 2 months to build. By now, a solid review history justifies higher rates, and the market for AI services supports it.

Tactics:
- Package services (e.g., "Lead Gen + Outreach Copy" bundle)
- Target agencies and SaaS companies rather than individual buyers
- Pursue Upwork fixed-price contracts over $300
- Apply for Fiverr Pro consideration once the portfolio is strong enough
